Anyone trying to describe the flavor of Mediterranean cuisine would start by mentioning the herbs and spices typically used. The fragrance, flavor and of course its wonderful aromatic spices make it exceptional. The herbs and spices are not only the foundation for this type of cooking they are the cornerstone that makes this cuisine unique. Our passion for Mediterranean food along with our experience for taste will stimulate your palate. At Mashawi Grill we are committed to providing the freshest, best quality Mediterranean cuisine, while at the same time preparing things that are healthy for you from our kitchen. This means we are dedicated to providing the highest quality USDA choice meats, fresh fish, free range poultry - with no hormones or artificial preservatives and of course the freshest vegetables from our local markets. Come and try Mashawi Grill today, we guarantee you will come back time and time again…
